This is a very simple demonstration of the annotation process.

When you join our Telegram group and ask for a pack, we will send you a link to a screen just like this one.

In the top left corner, click LOGIN, and enter as username and password: cord19 

Now you can start adding annotations. Simply select or doble click a word, and select the right annotation. To add relations, click and drag from one annotated span to another and then select the relation.

> SARS-CoV 2 is a novel coronavirus that causes the COVID-19 disease

Take your time to read each sentence and carefully understand and annotate only the MOST RELEVANT information. It is a hard and often subjective process, so humans are absolutely necessary!
